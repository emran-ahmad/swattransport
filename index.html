Core Features

Everything You Need to Run
a Modern Transport Business

From daily trip assignments to monthly P&L reports, HVTMS covers every operational and financial aspect of your transport company.

🚛

Fleet Management

Track owned vehicles and manage hired contractors in one unified dashboard. Monitor vehicle status, assignments, and maintenance history with ease.

📋

Trip Tracking & Assignment

Assign trips to drivers on owned vehicles or outsource to hired contractors. Log trip details, routes, and completion status in real time.

💰

Driver Payroll

Automate salary calculations including base pay, advances, held salary, overtime, and deductions. Generate payslips for every driver instantly.

🧾

VAT-Compliant Invoicing

Issue Fatoorah-ready invoices with 15% VAT, client details, and itemized services. Fully compliant with ZATCA requirements for Saudi Arabia.

📊

P&L Reporting

View your business health in both accrual and cash accounting modes. Drill into revenue, expenses, owner draws, and net profit at any time.

🤝

Hired Vehicle Settlements

Reconcile payments with hired contractors, track commissions, and manage outstanding balances — all without spreadsheets or manual calculation.
